Welcome to Our Parish

A Community of Faith, Love, and Service

St Charles Lwanga Jinja Kaloli Parish is a vibrant faith community dedicated to worship, spiritual growth, and service to the people of Jinja Kaloli and surrounding communities. We invite you to join us in our journey of faith.

Our Mission

To build a Christ-centered community rooted in faith, love, and service.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and a witness to the Gospel in our community.
